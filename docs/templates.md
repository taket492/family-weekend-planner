Templates and Auto-generation (Beta)

- Three templates: 半日(3スポット), 1日(5スポット), 雨天(屋内中心)
- Uses current search results as the candidate pool; optional budget filter.
- Generated selection is pushed to PlanBuilder for editing and saving.

Notes
- No server persistence of templates yet; local-only flow.
- Generation is greedy from the top results; refine later with profile/constraints.
